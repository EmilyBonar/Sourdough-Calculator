Sourdough Calculator

Like a lot of people, I made a sourdough starter during COVID-19. When I went to bake with it, I ran into recipes that called for starters of different stiffness, or flour/water ratio. This app caluclates the amount of flour, water, and starter to use in a recipe depending on your starter's hydration level and what the recipe calls for.
